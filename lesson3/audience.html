Target Audience

The target audience is the ages between 15-60,or who anyone else that would like to know the infos about the weather in their cities. Those people have interest about this information to go to school, to work and etc.

Personas

• Alessandra is a 26 years old engineer who lives in a good neighborhood. She go to work everyday by car, She lives in a sunny city, but she knows that when rain the traffic is chaotic, because of that, she is always aware about the weather.

• Gustavo is 35 years old and he is a pilot. He flies everyday but most of his flights happens in Brazil. The company where he is the pilot provide to him all the weather forecast, he likes to research by himself.

Scenarios

• 1: Alessandra wakes up and see that it is raining, she knows that because of the rain, she need to hurry up to go to work, but more important than that, she needs to know if the rain will be present in the city all day.

• 2: Gustavo will flies to Brazil's Northeast and he will get two days off to enjoy the place. The Brazil's Northeast is very hot but to be sure, he will check it.

• 3: Alessandra will travel to the south in the weekend, she will go by car so she needs to know the weather to the trip and to enjoy the city.

• 4: Gustavo has a meeting in Sao Paulo in two days, he knows that Sao Paulo is an instable city, so he needs to check the weather to plan the best route.

• 5: Alessandra will have a dinner in a friend's house, and it's a sunny day, but at night she thinks that it's gonna to be kind of cold, so she will check it on the site.
